Welcome to Read My Paper!

Read My Paper is a web application for peer editing

with Read My Paper, students of Trinity College can hone their writing skills while helping their peers with academic papers.

Read My Paper makes it easy and convenient for students to have access to peer editing.

If you are a student of Trinity College, please register with your Trinity College email account.

About This Website

Read My Paper was developed by three Trinity College computer science students for CPSC340, Software Engineering.

This website was developed using the Django framework for Python as the final project for the course.

This website was also developed using Agile methodologies.

Many thanks to all of our classmates for their continued support and to Professor Budd for his guidance.
